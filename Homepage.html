      Machine Learning, explainable AI
  • Progetto In codice Ratio
    • Knowledge extraction from medieval manuscripts
      Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision
  • Progetto CERTA (in collaborazione con AT&T Research e University of Toronto)
